Rrivacy

Welcome to Google-Calendar. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our calendar service to create events.

1. Information Collected

When you create events on our calendar, we collect the following types of information:

Personal information: such as your name and email address.

Event details: including event title, date, time, description, location, and recurrence patterns.

Invited users' email addresses.

2. How Information is Used

We use the collected information to:

Display events to the user who created them.

Send event invitations to invited users via email.

Provide reminders or notifications about upcoming events.

Analyze user behavior to improve the application's features and user experience.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties except as necessary to provide the calendar service, such as sending email invitations.

Any sharing of data with third parties is done with explicit user consent or as required by law.

4. Data Retention

Events may be retained indefinitely unless deleted by the user. User account information is retained as long as the user maintains an account with the application.

5. User Control and Rights

Users have the right to access, update, or delete their account and event data. They can also control notifications or opt-out of certain data processing activities.

6. Security Measures

We implement security measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. These measures include encryption of data during transmission and storage, access controls, and regular security audits.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically, and users will be notified of any significant changes.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at husakmaria74@gmail.com.

9. Consent

By using the Application and creating events on the calendar, you consent to the collection, processing, and use of your information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
